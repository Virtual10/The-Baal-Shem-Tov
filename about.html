About

This sites space is dedicated to collecting and making all texts and scholarship relating to the first generations of Chassidism available to the interested reader. Here you will find early manuscripts, first-edition to later-edition works by some of the disciples of the Baal Shem Tov and their student-successors. In addition, more recent scholarly and historical studies/interpretations of these works, their authors and their times are also made available. Readers may comment and exchange their appreciation and understanding of this burgeoning field on the BaalShem’s teachings and those in his circle of disciples.
